## 📌 Project Overview

The Pizza Sales Dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful and easy-to-understand visual insights. Instead of analyzing complex tables or spreadsheets, users can quickly interpret business performance through interactive charts and KPIs.

The dashboard helps users understand the **overall business health** by summarizing key metrics such as total revenue, total orders, and average order value. These indicators provide a quick snapshot of how well the business is performing at any given time.

By visualizing **daily and monthly trends**, users can identify patterns such as peak days and seasonal demand. This enables better planning for staffing, inventory management, and marketing campaigns during high-demand periods.

The **sales distribution charts** (by category and size) allow users to clearly see customer preferences. For example, identifying which pizza category or size is most popular helps businesses focus on high-demand products and optimize their menu offerings.

The **best and worst seller analysis** is especially useful for decision-making. It highlights top-performing pizzas that can be promoted further, as well as underperforming items that may need improvement, discounts, or removal from the menu.

Overall, the dashboard enables:
-Faster and more accurate decision-making
-Identification of sales opportunities and risks
- Improved product and inventory management
- Better understanding of customer behavior

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze key sales metrics (Revenue, Orders, Quantity)
- Identify **sales trends over time**
- Understand **customer preferences**
- Highlight **best and worst-selling products**
